## Create Your First Business Video Call

### Step 1: Open Event Creator
1. Go to **Home page** (http://localhost:3000)
2. Look for the **Create Post** section
3. Click the **ğŸ“… Event** button (blue background)

### Step 2: Fill Event Details (Step 1/3)
- Choose event type: **ğŸ“¹ Video Call** (or Meeting, Interview, etc.)
- Enter title: e.g., "Client Strategy Meeting"
- Add description (optional)
- Click **Next**

### Step 3: Schedule Meeting (Step 2/3)
- Select **date** from calendar
- Choose **start time**
- Set **duration** (15, 30, 45, 60 min buttons or custom)
- Select **video platform**: Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet
- Paste **meeting link** (optional)
- Click **Next**

### Step 4: Set Reminders (Step 3/3)
- Default reminders already set:
  * 15 minutes before
  * 1 hour before
  * 24 hours before
- Add custom reminders if needed
- Click **Create Event**

### Step 5: Get Reminders
- System automatically checks every minute
- You'll see notifications based on time:
  * **2 hours before**: Info notification (blue)
  * **15 minutes before**: Warning notification (yellow)
  * **5 minutes before**: Urgent notification (red) + Toast popup

### Step 6: Join Meeting
- When reminder appears, click **"Join Call"** button
- Meeting opens in new tab
- Or click **"Dismiss"** to hide reminder
